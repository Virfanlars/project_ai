Sepsis Early Warning System Analysis Report

System Overview

This system is based on real clinical data from the MIMIC-IV database and uses a multimodal deep learning model to predict the risk of patients developing sepsis. The system integrates vital signs, laboratory tests, medication use, and clinical text records to provide real-time risk scores and explainable warning results.

Clinical Application Recommendations

Based on the results of this system, we recommend:

  • Increase monitoring frequency for patients with risk scores above 0.7
  • Consider immediate intervention when a patient's risk score exceeds 0.9
  • Pay special attention to changes in key indicators like lactate levels, white blood cell count, temperature, and blood pressure
  • Combine the risk trajectories provided by the system with clinical judgment to develop personalized treatment plans
